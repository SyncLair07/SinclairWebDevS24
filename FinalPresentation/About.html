Welcome to UrbanSifter

Uncover, Preserve, Share

Our Mission

At UrbanSifter, we're driven by a passion for uncovering hidden treasures in daily disposables. Our platform serves as a dynamic community for enthusiasts of photography, city walk, stooping, dumpster diving, beach combing, and mud larking. We believe every discarded item has a story and, perhaps, a second chance at life. Through our map and list functionalities, we aim to connect like-minded individuals, share valuable information, and make a tangible impact on reducing waste.

How It Works

  1. Explore: Use our interactive map to discover spots near you where treasures await. From urban landscapes to serene beaches, the world is full of hidden gems waiting to be found.
  2. Share: Found something interesting? Share your find with the community! Upload photos, location details, and your story.
  3. Learn: Our list function offers a curated database of locations, tips, and how-to guides. If you would like to learn more about your city through the collective history told by trash, feel free to check out our ARCHIVE

Safety First

Your safety is paramount. Always:

  1. Wear appropriate clothing and protective gear.
  2. Be aware of your surroundings and local regulations.
  3. Never venture into dangerous areas alone.
  4. If you come across information on our platform that seems misleading, incorrect, or potentially harmful, please report it to our moderation team.
